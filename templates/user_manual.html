Section 1

Introduction

TeaCare AI is a smart system that helps tea farmers quickly detect diseases on tea leaves, get accurate treatment recommendations, and track the recovery progress of the same leaf over time.

Main Features

Create an account and manage your fields
Upload a tea leaf photo for instant disease analysis
Receive clear symptoms, treatments, and practical tips
Track recovery using the same Barcode / Plant ID

Section 2

How to Get Started

Step 1 — Create an Account
1

Open the Application

Launch TeaCare AI in your web browser or mobile device.

2

Click Sign Up

If you don't have an account yet, tap Sign Up on the landing page.

3

Fill in Your Details

First Name Last Name Email Address Phone (optional) Password
4

Click Create Account

Step 2 — Login
1

Enter your credentials

Type your registered Email Address and Password.

2

Click Login

Section 3

Adding a Field

Before analyzing a leaf, you must create at least one field. Fields help you organise scans by location and plot.

1

Go to Add Field

Click Add Field in the top navigation menu.

2

Fill in the field details

📍 Field Name (e.g. Nuwara Eliya Block A) 🗺 Location
3

Click Save Field

Your field is now saved and ready to use in the Analyzer.

Section 4

Tea Plant Analysis Process

Disease detection + treatment recommendation in a few clicks.

1

Navigate to Analyze

Click Analyze in the top navigation menu to open the Plant Analyzer.

2

Upload a Tea Leaf Photo

Drag & drop a clear photo into the upload box, or click Browse. Supports JPG, PNG, and JPEG.

3

Enter the required details

🌱 Field 📍 Location (or auto-detect) 🏷 Barcode / Plant ID

The Barcode / Plant ID is critical — it links future scans of the same leaf for Recovery Tracking.

4

Click Start Analysis

Hit the green Start Analysis button and wait for results.

Section 5

Recovery Tracker

If you analyze the same leaf again later using the same Barcode / Plant ID, the system automatically recognises it and tracks how well the leaf is healing after treatment.

Section 7

Safety Note & Best Practices

Use clear, well-lit photos

Shoot in good natural light with the full leaf visible and in focus. Blurry or dark photos reduce detection accuracy.

Consult a local agricultural officer

TeaCare AI provides recommendations based on standard practices. Always verify with a qualified expert before applying any chemicals.

Keep the same Barcode / Plant ID per leaf

Reusing the same ID every time you re-scan a leaf is essential for the Recovery Tracker to show accurate progress over time.
